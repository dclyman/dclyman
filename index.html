I'm A Writer By Heart, But A Web Designer By Necessity.

BY: DYLAN LYMAN

While many profess to be web dsesigners, I am not here to claim I am a virtuoso of web development. However, I do want to understand more, and use my already present knowledge of web development to augment my writing. I see wordsmithing in the 21st century as an embedded profession of the internet world. While, in the past, print media reigned above all else as an entry for an aspiring writer, today the web has become the newspaper, the television, and the silver screen all in one. So, understanding the platform in which my writing will reside is important to me.
